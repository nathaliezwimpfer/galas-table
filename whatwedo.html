Our Process

We ask questions and we listen. We dig deep to understand the unique complexities of your challenge. We reflect on the creative practices of visual artists within our vast network and consider the intersections and adjacencies between their practices and your challenge. We find the perfect fit and celebrate unexpected collaborations. Together, we generate transformative ideas and results.

What We Do

We are committed to a more equitable and sustainable world and therefore engage in projects and partnerships that share our goals and values. We are currently focused on the following:

Sustainable Product Design and Development

We bring artists to the table to envision the possibilities within your waste streams, from designing upcycled products to be re-introduced to consumer markets, to utilizing materials that can be distributed for social good.

Think Tables

We bring artists to your table to facilitate collaborative sessions that foster creative dialogue around developing concepts and designing solutions related to specific social impact themes or challenges.
