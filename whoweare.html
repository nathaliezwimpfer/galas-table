Gala’s Table brings proven leadership from the contemporary art world, deep relationships with a vast network of international artists, and expertise in sustainability and consumer insights.

Curatorial Janine Foeller and Micky Schubert have more than 35 years of collective knowledge and experience in the field of contemporary art. They have run critically acclaimed gallery spaces in NYC and Berlin, organized numerous exhibitions, founded experimental spaces and publications and continue to be actively engaged with artists and the ever-growing, global art community.

Business and Consumer Insights Simona Rubin has extensive experience in working with a diverse range of companies across sectors, to lead and design research plans, execute data analyses, and develop actionable business strategies and services.

Sustainability and Resilience Siobhan Foley has worked with businesses, governments, and nonprofits for more than 15 years on numerous programs, policies, and products to address the climate crisis, accelerate the deployment of advanced energy solutions, and support development of the circular economy.

Our Name

The company’s name was inspired by Les Diners de Gala, a “cookbook” created by the Spanish Surrealist artist Salvador Dali and originally published in 1973. The book is part cookbook and part artist book, where recipes share pages with Dali’s drawings as well as photographs of the surrealist dinner parties that he and Gala—his wife and life-long muse—hosted. Les Diners de Gala stands as a visual testament to how the table can be a catalyst for communion and creativity, where there is a space for a multitude of views and voices.
