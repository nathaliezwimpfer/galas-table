At Gala’s Table, we foster collaborations between organizations and contemporary artists to generate ideas and innovative solutions that otherwise would not be realized.

We are committed to a more equitable and sustainable world and therefore engage in projects and partnerships that share our goals and values. We are open to exploring ideas large and small and are currently focusing on the following areas:

Sustainable Product Design and Development

We bring artists to the table to envision and develop the creative possibilities in your waste streams, such as designing upcycled products for consumer markets or utilizing materials in unexpected ways.

Think Tables

Artists rely on an open mind to tap into their creativity. We cultivate this openness at our Think Tables where artists meet you “at the table” for highly interactive sessions that create space for important questions, shift perspectives, and develop new concepts, strategies and design solutions.
